Client
Starbucks
Industry

Duration

Overview

As the world's largest coffeehouse chain with 35,000+ locations globally, Starbucks needed to understand why their loyalty app—despite millions of downloads—had declining retention rates. Our mission was to conduct comprehensive UX research to identify barriers and provide strategic recommendations to improve customer engagement and long-term retention.

Key Deliverables

  • Multi-phase UX research study with 120+ participants

11-month UX research initiative

Challenges

Approach

  • Conducted mixed-methods research: surveys, interviews, usability tests, analytics
  • Built interactive data visualizations to communicate behavioral patterns
  • Developed evidence-based strategic recommendations backed by user data
  • Delivered comprehensive research report with prioritized UX improvement roadmap

Critical insights from disaster response operations

Lack of Shared Situational Awareness
No single view exists showing the real-time state of active disasters. Teams rely on phone calls and emails to understand what's happening across locations.
