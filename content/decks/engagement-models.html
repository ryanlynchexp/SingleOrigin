How You Can Engage With Us

Three flexible models designed to match your needs, whether you need a single specialist, a full product team, or end-to-end delivery ownership.

1 Staffing 2 Managed Team 3 Managed Service
Model 1

Staffing

Individual professionals placed to fill your resource gaps. You retain day-to-day management and delivery responsibility while Experis handles sourcing, vetting, payroll, benefits, and administrative overhead.

Best For

Short-term needs, specialized skills, or scaling your existing teams quickly without the overhead of traditional hiring.

How It Works

Experis sources, vets, and places talent directly into your team. You manage the work, set priorities, and own delivery. We handle payroll, benefits, and all administrative overhead so you can focus on execution.

Typical Roles
  • UX Designers
  • UI Engineers
  • Front-end Developers
  • UX Researchers
  • Data Analysts
Model 2

Managed Team

A dedicated cross-functional team — design, development, QA, and project management — assembled and managed by Experis but fully integrated into your workflow, tools, and culture.

Best For

Ongoing product work, teams that need to scale fast without hiring, and clients who want Experis accountability with embedded collaboration.

How It Works

Experis assembles and manages the team end to end. You set priorities and product direction. We handle delivery cadence, quality assurance, and team performance — so you get the output without the management overhead.

Typical Composition
  • 1 Product Designer
  • 2-4 Developers
  • 1 QA Engineer
  • 1 Delivery Lead
Model 3

Managed Service

Outcome-based engagement where Experis owns delivery, quality, and performance metrics under a defined scope and SLA. You define what success looks like — we own the how.

Best For

Defined projects with clear success criteria, organizations that want to hand off execution entirely, and ongoing service contracts.

How It Works

You define outcomes and success metrics. Experis owns everything else — staffing, process, tools, and delivery timeline. We provide regular reporting against agreed KPIs so you always know where things stand.

Typical Engagements
  • Design System Builds
  • Platform Redesigns
  • Research Programs
  • Accessibility Audits

Which Model Is Right for You?

Start with where you are today.

Choose Staffing If…

You need specific skills, fast

  • You have an existing team that needs reinforcement
  • You want to manage the work directly
  • The need is short-term or project-specific
  • You need to move in days, not weeks
Choose Managed Team If…

You need a whole team, with built-in leadership

  • You have ongoing product work without enough headcount
  • You want Experis to handle team management
  • You need design, dev, and QA working together
  • You want embedded collaboration, not outsourcing
Choose Managed Service If…

You want to hand off execution entirely

  • You have a defined project with clear success metrics
  • You want SLA-backed delivery accountability
  • You prefer to manage outcomes, not people
  • You need predictable cost and timeline
