Design a modern web platform called Experis Proposal Studio used internally to build interactive client proposal experiences and externally to present proposal responses through a narrative microsite.

The design should feel future-state Experis branding: light mode, modern SaaS aesthetic, subtle gradients, soft glass effects, minimal UI chrome, and confident enterprise tech design. The interface should feel advanced, intelligent, and premium, avoiding anything that resembles traditional PDF or slide decks.

Use a story-driven microsite structure for the client-facing experience, combined with top navigation tabs and scroll-based anchored sections.

The system consists of two core products:
	1.	Internal Workspace (web app)
	2.	Client Proposal Experience (published microsite)

Design both.

⸻

Product Concept

Experis Proposal Studio is a proposal intelligence and storytelling platform that transforms traditional RFP responses into interactive digital proposal experiences.

Internal teams use the workspace to:

• store RFP documents
• collaborate on responses
• model pricing and staffing
• build proposal narrative sections
• assemble client-facing experiences

When ready, a shareable URL is generated which presents the proposal as a clean, interactive proposal microsite.

Clients navigate the proposal like a product experience rather than reading a document.
